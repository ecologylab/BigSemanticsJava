<div class="h4" style="clear:all; margin-top: -18px;" >IdeaM&Acirc;CH&Eacute;</div>

	<div class="feature"style="padding-bottom:6px">
		<a href="http://ideamache.ecologylab.net" target="_blank">
			<!--
				<img height="100" src="images/ideamache_logo+title_transparent.png" style="float:left; padding-right:12px;"/>
			-->
			<img height="154" src="http://ideamache.ecologylab.net/static/mache/resources/branding/logo_huge.png" style="float:left; padding-right:12px;"/>
		</a>
		<a target="_blank" class="destination"
 href="http://ideamache.ecologylab.net">IdeaM&Acirc;CH&Eacute;</a> is
a web browser and cloud-based creativity support tool that uses information composition as a medium of curation.
<i>Curation</i> is the process of assembling information in meaningful exhibits, for people to think about.
Curation involges finding, choosing, collecting, arranging, annotating and synthesizing information to connect and form ideas.
In <a target="_blank" class="destination" href="http://ideamache.ecologylab.net">IdeaM&Acirc;CH&Eacute;</a>,
users gather clippings, which automatically become enriched with metadata and so are represented as <i>rich bookmarks</i>.
The metadata includes descriptions and images, as well as authors, categories, and citations of articles and patents,
and price, ratings and bestseller rankins of products from vendors such as Amazon and HomeDepot.
Expressive operations on clippings can create a visual sense of connectedness among curated elements.
 </p>
